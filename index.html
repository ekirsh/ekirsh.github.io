Vaginal Ring.


The Vaginal Ring
is a effective way
to stop pregnancy

It does not stop STI's

How does it work?

The vaginal ring is inserted into the vagina. The ring then releases hormones such as estrogen and progestin. The hormones are then absorbed by the vagina which stops the ovary from releasing an egg and makes it more difficult for the sperm to travel into the uterus.

What are the side effects?

The side effects may include upset stomachs, bleeding between periods and headaches. It also may cause vaginal irritation and might slip out under certain circumstances.

Where can I buy it?

To buy a Vaginal Ring, you need to see your doctor.
